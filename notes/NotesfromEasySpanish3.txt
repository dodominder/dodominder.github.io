Chapter	3: Hay, Interrogatives words, days, months

HAY!
el, la, los, las never follows hay.

Examples of use
Hay una alfombra en la casa 	There is a carpet in the house
Hay un árbol en el jardin	There is a tree in the garden
Hay dos vasos en la mesa	There are two glasses on the table
Hay tres libros en el piso	There are three books on the floor

When hay is followed by a plural noun, the article is omitted
Hay tigres en el zoológico	There are tigers in the zoo
Hay estrellas en el cielo	There are stars in the sky

To make a sentence negative add no before hay
No hay luz en el baño 		There is no light in the bathroom

All interrogatives carry accents but these accents do not change the pronunciation
Cómo		 How
Dónde		 Where	
Quién, Quiénes	 Who
Qué		 What
Cuál, Cuáles	 Which
Por qué		 Why
Cuánto		 How much
Cuántos, Cuántas How many
