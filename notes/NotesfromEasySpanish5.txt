Regular Verbs

All Spanish verbs belong to one of three conjugations
-ar, -er, -ir 

VERB STEM + infinitive ending = Infinitive 

cant + ar = cantar	to sing
com + er = comer	to eat
viv + ir = vivir	to live

Verbs are regular if the stem doesn't change when conjugated

Present Tense
Ella canta una conción triste	She sings a sad song, She is singing a sad song

¿Cantas tú los domingos?	Do you sing on Sundays?
¿Tú cantas los domingos?	You sing on Sundays?

The present tense can express the future when combined with an adverbial expression
Ella canta con usted mañana	She'll sing with you tomorrow

Add no before a verb to negate it.
