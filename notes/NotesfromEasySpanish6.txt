Chapter 6:

Irregular Verbs

Verbs are irregular if the stem changes when the word is conjugated.
NOSOTROS y VOSOTROS forms are unaffected (in present tense).

KEY TAKE AWAY: 	For -ar: e->ie, o->ue, u->ue
		For -er: e->ie, o->ue
		For -ir: e->ie, o->ue, e->i
